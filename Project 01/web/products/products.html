SOFTLINE 82 MD

An innovative energy saving system that meets the requirements of the future

SOFTLINE 82 meets all requirements for safety, functionality and thermal insulation. The geometric profile of this innovative system is thermally reinforced and guarantees optimal heat insulation.

Thanks to the unique VEKA quality and the wall thickness of the profile in class A according to the RAL certificate, the SOFTLINE 82 system provides optimal stability even in large elements such as glass walls that must meet high static requirements.

Elegant design for the best thermal insulation.

Thanks to the elegant design of the profile, the SOFTLINE 82 system lets a large amount of light into the rooms, but is still very economical. Thanks to the high stability of the system and its superior thermal insulation, it is possible to connect the requirements of modern architecture for large glass surfaces and maximum energy efficiency.

In terms of shape and color, the SOFTLINE 82 system does not set limits for your creative freedom.

Security doors

Standart model security door

3 points of locking
  • metal structure that is made of steel profiles 20X40, the inside of the structure is filled with fully pressed tervol d=40mm
  • thermo-sound and fire-resistant insulation for a duration of 30 minutes
  • the profiles form a grid that is dotted on both sides with a decanted sheet 2x = 1.00mm
  • metal construction is coated on both sides with veneered plywood = 16mm, the color and applied carving is at the customer's choice, with a wide range of colors available (white, walnut, mahogany, cherry, bush, etc.) as well as applications of carvings

The door is equipped with a Securemme (Italy) model 2530 security system, for multi-sided locking with cash keys and an ABUS (Germany) dot-coded cylinder.

Locking with the security system is at 5 points at the lock, at 2 points low down side with deviator, at the top vertically at 1 point and from the back (at the hinges) at 3 points with fixed bolts.

When the door is closed, the fixed bolts go directly into the wall through the cash register.

The metal door frame is made of galvanized sheet metal ^2.00 mm, electrostatically painted in the color of the customer's choice.

In the basic shape of the security door, a magic eye with a field of view of 160° is incorporated, the handles and rosettes can be made of matte chrome.

These elements are a product of Securemme (Italy).

Mosquito nets

As a complement to the program of exterior carpentry, we offer mosquito nets that are placed in the form of a net on the windows and doors in your home.

In this way, it is possible to prevent the entry of insects.

After installing the mosquito nets, the windows or doors can be opened or closed continuously.

The frame of the mosquito net is made of aluminum and the net of glass fibers, i.e. fiberglass. Fiberglass has a high resistance to external influences, which increases its service life. These mosquito nets provide 100% efficiency in preventing the entry of insects.
