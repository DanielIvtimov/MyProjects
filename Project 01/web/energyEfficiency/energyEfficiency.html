Energy Efficiency

Energy efficiency is the performance of the same or greater amount of activities with the same or less amount of consumed energy (thermal, light, kinetic) and with less emission of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere.

WHAT IS RENEWABLE ENERGY?

Renewable energy is energy that is renewed quickly through natural processes. Renewable energy sources are sun, wind, water, geothermal or biomass.

The advantage of these resources is that they are in unlimited quantities and their use does not pollute the environment.

ENERGY EFFICIENT CARPENTRY

Windows, doors and skylights are an integral part of residential buildings, the window is a mandatory opening of the building with the basic function of letting daylight into the interior of the building and enabling natural ventilation of the premises.

The total losses through the windows is 50% of the building's heat loss. They are usually ten times greater than the heat loss through the walls, which is why the importance of energy-efficient windows in the total energy needs of the building is clear. In addition to reducing heating costs, energy-efficient windows contribute to increasing the comfort of living and working.

If the windows and doors are made of materials that do not meet certain quality requirements and are improperly installed, they can be the cause of significant heat losses, condensation, mold and poor comfort in the home.

The loss of heat through the windows is transferable, ie through a closed window and ventilation and through an open window. When both are collected, more than 50% of the total heat loss of the building is realized through the windows.

There are different types of windows and their coefficients of thermal conductivity range from 0.8 W / m2K with the best windows with triple glass and gas filling to 3.5 W / m2K in old wooden windows. It is recommended that the overall heat transfer coefficient does not exceed 1.8 W / m2K.

Quality windows reduce the transmission of heat loss, and losses through ventilation can be reduced by installing ventilation systems. In this way, ventilation losses are reduced.

ADVANTAGES OF A PROPERLY INSTALLED NEW, ENERGY EFFICIENT PRODUCT:

  • reduces energy consumption and saves money,
  • increases comfort in the home,
  • reduces condensation on windows in cold weather,
  • reduces noise penetration.

High quality windows, doors and skylights are much more efficient than products 25 years ago. They seal better, have low glass emissivity, inert gas or vacuum between the glasses and better designed frames.

Energy-efficient products make the home more comfortable, because there is no draft, the indoor air has higher humidity in the heating season, and in the summer the freshness is maintained.

RIn order to make it easier for consumers to choose and to guarantee that they have chosen a quality product, it is already a practice for carpentry to be certified and carry appropriate labels that confirm their characteristics.

A CERTIFIED PRODUCT THAT HAS A QUALITY LABEL SATISFIES THE FOLLOWING CHARACTERISTICS:

  • Double or triple glazing, with sealed insulating glass panel
  • Low emissivity glass
  • Filling the glass with argon or krypton
  • Low conductivitys
  • Good sealing

INSTALLATION OF CARPENTRY

Joinery (windows, doors, skylights) should be installed by skilled persons, according to the manufacturer's instructions. Poorly installed products (even if the product itself is of good quality) do not work properly and can create cold drafts, water leaks, mechanism breakdown and window deformation.
