For Us

The company OptiWindow DOOEL Skopje is privately owned. The owner and manager of the company is Mr. Zlatko Spasoski. The headquarters and the production plant are located in Volkovo street, and the showroom is located at 1 Varshavska street (in the Nastel Taftalidze Karposh building)

After 10 years of successful operation on the market in the exhibition hall which was located at 70 b Partizanski ul. in the city of Alumnika in Karposh, on June 16/2021 in the presence of its business partners, associates and customers, the new showroom was solemnly opened at 1 Varshavska St. in the Nastel building, Taftalidze.

The showroom is well-appointed and easily accessible, with an exhibition space of about 100 square meters where customers can view the products on display. There are window systems with classic opening, to those that meet the strict requirements of passive objects, as well as sliding systems - lift-and-slide systems that are distinguished by a minimalist design and superior features. Of course, here you can also look at a wide selection of entrance security doors for houses or apartments, which, in addition to the basic function, are designed to refine the space and give a completely new dimension to everyone's home.

We, as a team, with this idea for this space, are trying to bring all our trump cards with which we perform on the market closer and within reach, and to have almost the entire offer "in the first person" in one place.

All types of systems and products are available in color with basic material to systems and products made in special Spectral colors - products from the Exclusive program.

The desire, trust, demands and needs of customers is the central orientation and main focus of our business. The flexibility and customized solutions of part of the products is our strong point, which makes us easily adaptable to the individual requirements of niche customers. At the same time, we as the company respect the requirements regarding controlled and quality production, timely service and after-sales comprehensive service.

The many years of successful operation in the market is the result of a mix of mutual trust, respect, a friendly relationship with customers and suppliers and a socially responsible attitude towards the community. From here, the goal of the newly opened showroom is to create a place and space where the solutions of our team and the requests of the customers will be met.

Whenever you have a new project, reconstruct an old building or build a new building, visit the showroom and there you will find a specialized customer support team, always ready to find the best technical solution.

The main activity of the company is the production and installation of

  • PVC windows and doors
  • Security doors
  • Entrance doors to the house
  • Aluminum carpentry for business buildings
  • PVC and aluminum blinds
  • Mosquito nets

Social responsibility of the company

At Vector Pro, we believe that the success of a company is not only measured by profit and market leadership, but also by how responsible the company is towards the community. That's why we strive for our activities to be based on responsible work practices in order to justify the trust of customers and the community in which we operate.

Responsible operation, care for our employees, the community and the environment in which we exist are extremely important segments of our company's strategy. We are dedicated to developing a multidimensional social responsibility program that covers a wide range of areas inside and outside the company. With this, we fulfill our vision, to leave a significant mark on the community, but also to be a good corporate citizen and an exemplary company.
